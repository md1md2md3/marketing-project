# WEBSITE STRUCTURE & UX

## Page 1: Homepage (The Hook)

- **Hero Section:** Full-screen holographic video. Headline: "AURA: Sentient Living."
- **Manifesto:** "Technology for Humanity" text block.
- **Interactive Diagram:** A central "AURA Unified" hub connected to 5 orbiting nodes (The Pillars).
- **Services Teaser:** Links to "The Vision Session" (Bookings).
- **Footer:** Legal Mentions (Mandatory).

## Page 2: Ecosystems (The Education)

- **Layout:** Vertical "Zig-Zag" Strips.
- **Content:** Deep dive into the 5 Pillars (Nexus, Sanctuary, Harmony, Flow, Source).
- **CTA:** "Shop [Pillar Name]" buttons linking to specific collections.

## Page 3: Shop (The Conversion)

- **Collections:**
  1.  NEXUS (Connectivity)
  2.  SANCTUARY (Security)
  3.  HARMONY (Comfort)
  4.  FLOW (Access)
  5.  SOURCE (Energy)
- **Strategy:** Sell high-ticket "Bundles" rather than single items.

## Page 4: Services (The Expert)

- **Booking System:** 3 distinct tiers of service (Design, Technical Audit, Privacy Setup).
